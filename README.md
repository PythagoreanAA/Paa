<h1>Pythagorean Arithmosophic Anamnesis</h1>
<p class="muted">A symbolic systems laboratory for structural coherence, orientation, and long-term intelligibility.</p>

<hr/>

<h2>What This Is</h2>
<p>
This practice works with <strong>number, symbol, proportion, and narrative structure</strong>
as diagnostic instruments rather than beliefs, aesthetics, or abstractions.
</p>

<p>
The aim is not insight, persuasion, or optimization —
but the restoration of <em>coherence</em> where systems have become misaligned,
overgrown, or unintentionally distorted.
</p>

<div class="callout">
Number here is treated as a cognitive scaffold —
a way meaning survives scale, time, and translation.
</div>

<hr/>

<h2>What I Diagnose</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Symbolic drift and narrative incoherence</li>
  <li>Misalignment between stated values and operative structures</li>
  <li>Hidden recursion, leakage, or collapse in conceptual systems</li>
  <li>Over-complexity masking structural absence</li>
  <li>Loss of orientation across time, scale, or audience</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>The Diagnostic Call</h2>
<p>
A diagnostic call is not a consultation, pitch, or coaching session.
It is a <strong>structural reading</strong>.
</p>

<p>
You present a system — organizational, cultural, conceptual, or symbolic.
I listen for proportion, symmetry, recursion, and fracture.
</p>

<ul>
  <li>No preparatory material required</li>
  <li>No obligation beyond the call</li>
  <li>No ideological or therapeutic framing</li>
</ul>

<p>
The output is clarity: what is structurally present, what is missing,
and what cannot persist as currently arranged.
</p>

<hr/>

<h2>Who This Is For</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Institutions stewarding meaning over long time spans</li>
  <li>Design and research organizations working pre-language</li>
  <li>Founders sensing coherence loss but lacking vocabulary</li>
  <li>Cultural or archival projects concerned with continuity</li>
</ul>
