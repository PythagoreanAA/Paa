The Foundation

PAA does not treat number as entertainment, affirmation, or personality theatre.

Pythagorean means number is regarded as archetypal structure: the sacred geometric grammar governing relation, tension, harmony, order and consequence.

Arithmosophic means wisdom through number: the disciplined recognition of number as fields of constraint, qualitative essence, archetypal tendency, and symbolic structure.

Anamnesis means remembering: recovering the pattern already operating beneath conscious awareness.

The aim of PAA is Hieroanamnesis — sacred remembrance through structural recognition. Understanding your Birth Root Glyph is the first threshold.
