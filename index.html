  • Structure over persuasion
  • Durability over aesthetics

What This Is

This practice works with number, symbol, proportion, and narrative structure as diagnostic instruments.

It does not ask for belief. It tests whether the system carries its own weight.

When a system becomes misaligned, it compensates with language, complexity, and narrative noise.

The reading identifies where structure has been replaced by decoration—and where recursion has turned parasitic.

What I Diagnose

  • Symbolic drift and narrative incoherence
  • Misalignment between stated values and operative structures
  • Hidden recursion, leakage, or collapse in conceptual systems
  • Over-complexity masking structural absence
  • Loss of orientation across time, scale, or audience

Common symptom: the system needs constant explanation to feel “true.” That usually means structure is missing and language is compensating.

A coherent system explains itself by its relations, not by insistence.
