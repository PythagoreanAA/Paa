About the Method

PAA — Pythagorean Arithmosophic Anamnesis — reads number as an archetypal structure rather than decoration. The birth root glyph is interpreted through number essence, virtue, vice, sacred geometry, and the EIEIA diagnostic sequence.

EIEIA separates five dimensions that are often collapsed into one vague impression: Essence, Impulse, Energy, Intelligence, and Archetypal Action. The final action must emerge from the prior four. That “therefore” is the blade.

This tool is for symbolic reflection and archetypal self-inquiry. It is not a scientific, medical, psychological, financial, or legal diagnosis.
