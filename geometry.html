Geometric Insight

A number becomes intelligible when its relation is made visible: the Monad as center, the Dyad as line, the Triad as plane, the Tetrad as frame, the Pentad as living proportion, the Hexad as reciprocity, the Heptad as resistant mystery, the Octad as octave-return, the Ennead as completion, and the Undecad as excess beyond closure.
